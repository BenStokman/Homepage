Tim Apple is Secretly Aiding Terrorists

This is pourposeful fake news/satire. Please do not take this seriously

By
Ben Stokman
January 9, 2020 at 6:02 PM EST

WASHINGTON — Tim Apple has announced said that Apple will not in any way assist in unlocking the devices of criminals, and terrorists.

My investigation concluded after I spent a minute (or even two) reading Donny Trumpy's tweet on the matter:

We are helping Apple all of the time on TRADE and so many other issues, and yet they refuse to unlock phones used by killers, drug dealers and other violent criminal elements. They will have to step up to the plate and help our great Country, NOW! MAKE AMERICA GREAT AGAIN.

— Donald J. Trump (@realDonaldTrump) January 14, 2020

This announcement comes at a time of high gun violence in this country; there are approximately 40,000 deadly shootings a year in this country, over 100 a day.

STUDY: Most Trumpy supporters are Republican

Apple has a history of obstructing investigations; Just in 2016 Apple refused to unlock a phone of a terrorist. Apple's obstruction of investigations has created a great obstacle for law enforcement all around the country. Police forces cannot get into any phone they want anymore - apple's hardware-level encryption prevents access to an iPhone or iPad in any way without the pin code; and the feature that wipes the phone after 10 passcode attemps makes it harder for law enforcement to extrapolate a passcode based off of security fottage where someone typed in their pin code.

On top of the obvious evidence that Apple is aiding terrorists, Apple has a vested interest in the downfall of Donny Trumpy. Trumpy's trade war has been a strain on Apple's supply chain, with almost all of Apple's products being made in Chineese sweatshops.

On top of that, Apple's headquarters are located in Sillicon valley, which is an area that is more accepting of Terrorists self-described faith.

So Apple is, without my doubt, aiding terrorists with its obstruction of police investigation.
